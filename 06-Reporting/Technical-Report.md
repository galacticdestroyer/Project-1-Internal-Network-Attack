
## Technical Report

### Attack Summary
The engagement followed a structured red team methodology:
1. Internal reconnaissance
2. Assumed breach initial access
3. Privilege escalation
4. Lateral movement
5. Post-exploitation and persistence

### Key Findings
- Active Directory misconfigurations enabled privilege escalation
- Credential reuse allowed rapid lateral movement
- Lack of monitoring enabled persistence

### Overall Impact
The environment was fully compromised within the engagement scope, demonstrating high organizational risk.
